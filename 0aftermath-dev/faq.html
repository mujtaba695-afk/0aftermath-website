What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

In 2026, SEO is evolving into GEO. AI models like SearchGPT and Perplexity don't just list websites; they summarize answers. GEO is the technical process of ensuring your brand is the primary source cited in those summaries. It requires structured semantic data and topical predominance rather than simple keyword stuffing.

How does predictive AI bidding outperform humans?

Humans react to yesterday's data; our AI predicts tomorrow's behavior. By analyzing micro-signals across multi-channel conversion paths, our systems adjust bids every 15 seconds to catch high-intent buyers before competitors even see the signal. This preemptive strikes typically reduces CAC by 40% compared to manual management.

What is an "Autonomous" CRM engine?

An autonomous CRM doesn't wait for your team to enter data. It uses AI agents to scrape intent, qualify leads through behavioral analysis, and trigger personalized follow-ups via SMS or Email instantly. It turns your database from a passive storage unit into a 24/7 self-optimizing sales engine.
